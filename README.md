# CityShop – Bridging Local Shops with Modern Commerce

CityShop is a city-focused commerce platform designed to merge traditional local shops with the convenience of e-commerce and quick commerce.

The goal is simple: make purchasing easy for customers while ensuring local shopkeepers remain profitable and relevant.

Instead of replacing local shops, CityShop strengthens them by providing digital visibility, marketing tools, and end-to-end delivery — all while preserving customer trust in familiar, nearby stores.

---

## Problem Statement

Local shopkeepers are steadily losing sales due to:

- The rapid rise of e-commerce and quick commerce platforms  
- Customer preference for fast, app-based purchasing  
- Lack of digital presence for small and traditional shops  

At the same time, customers benefit from:

- Easy ordering  
- Fast delivery  
- The ability to purchase from multiple sellers in one order  

Ignoring either side is unrealistic. CityShop addresses both.

---

## Solution Overview

CityShop combines:

- The trust and quality of local shops  
- The speed and convenience of quick commerce  

Customers can browse trusted shops in their city, purchase products from one or multiple shops, and receive fast delivery — while shopkeepers gain a digital storefront without losing ownership or control.

---

## User Roles

CityShop is built around three core user roles, each with clearly defined responsibilities and workflows.

### 1. Shopkeeper
Local business owners who register their physical shops on the platform to sell products digitally while retaining full control over pricing, inventory, and operations.

### 2. Customer
End users who browse local shops within their city, explore products, and place orders from one or multiple shops through a single checkout.

### 3. Delivery Partner
Independent delivery partners responsible for order pickup from shops and delivery to customers, operating through a real-time assignment system.

---

## Shopkeeper Workflow

### Subscription & Onboarding
- Shopkeepers subscribe to the platform to register their shop
- Shop verification ensures authenticity and trust

### Shop & Product Management
- Create and manage a digital storefront
- Add, update, or remove products with images and descriptions
- Update pricing and availability in real time
- Mark products as available or out of stock

### Marketing & Promotion
- Upload promotional images or videos
- Run in-app advertisements for products or the shop
- Increase visibility across the city

### Order Fulfillment
- Receive real-time notifications for new orders
- Prepare and pack orders
- Mark orders as packed for delivery handoff
- Delivery logistics are fully handled by the platform

---

## Customer Workflow

### Authentication & City Selection
- User logs in or registers
- Selects their city to view available local shops

### Shop Discovery
- Browse all registered shops in the selected city
- Choose shops based on familiarity and trust

### Product Exploration
- View detailed product listings with images and videos
- Watch promotional content uploaded by shopkeepers

### Ordering
- Add products from one or multiple shops
- Complete checkout with a single order

### Delivery
- Receive fast, quick-commerce-style delivery
- Track order status in real time

---

## Delivery Partner Workflow

### Login & Availability
- Delivery partners log in and set availability status
- Access live delivery requests within their city

### Order Notification
- All available partners receive delivery requests simultaneously
- Notifications include:
  - Pickup location(s)
  - Drop location
  - Delivery earnings

### Order Assignment Logic
- The first partner to accept the request is assigned the order
- Once accepted, the request is automatically disabled for others
- Prevents duplicate claims and conflicts

### Delivery Execution
- Pick up items from one or multiple shops
- Deliver the consolidated order to the customer
- Earnings are tracked per completed delivery
