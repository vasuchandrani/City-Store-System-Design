# CityShop – Bridging Local Shops with Modern Commerce

CityShop is a city-focused commerce platform designed to merge traditional local shops with the convenience of e-commerce and quick commerce.  
The goal is simple: **make purchasing easy for customers while ensuring local shopkeepers remain profitable and relevant**.

Instead of replacing local shops, CityShop strengthens them by providing digital visibility, marketing tools, and end-to-end delivery — all while preserving customer trust in familiar, nearby stores.

---

## Problem Statement

Local shopkeepers are steadily losing sales due to:
- The rapid rise of e-commerce and quick commerce platforms
- Customer preference for fast, app-based purchasing
- Lack of digital presence for small and traditional shops

At the same time, customers benefit from:
- Easy ordering
- Fast delivery
- The ability to purchase from multiple sellers in one order

Ignoring either side is unrealistic. CityShop addresses both.

---

## Solution Overview

CityShop combines:
- **The trust and quality of local shops**
- **The speed and convenience of quick commerce**

Customers can browse trusted shops in their city, purchase products from one or multiple shops, and receive fast delivery — while shopkeepers gain a digital storefront without losing ownership or control.

---

## User Roles

The application has **two user roles**:

### 1. Shopkeeper
Shop owners who list and manage their physical shops on the platform.

### 2. Customer
Buyers who browse local shops, view products, and place orders.

---

## Shopkeeper Flow

1. **Subscription & Registration**
   - Shopkeepers subscribe to register their shop on the platform.

2. **Shop & Product Management**
   - Add new products by uploading photos and details
   - Edit product name, description, and media
   - Remove products that are no longer sold
   - Mark products as *available* or *out of stock*

3. **Marketing**
   - Upload product marketing images or videos
   - Promote shop and products within the app

4. **Order Fulfillment**
   - Receive customer orders through the app
   - Platform handles end-to-end delivery from shop to customer

---

## Customer Flow

1. **Login & City Selection**
   - User logs in and selects their city

2. **Browse Shops**
   - View all registered shops in the selected city
   - Choose shops they already trust

3. **Explore Products**
   - View product photos, videos, and descriptions
   - Watch marketing content uploaded by shopkeepers

4. **Purchase**
   - Add products from **one or multiple shops**
   - Place a single order

5. **Delivery**
   - Receive products through fast, quick-commerce-style delivery

---

## Why Customers Use CityShop

- Buy from **shops they already trust**
- Get **quality assurance and satisfaction**
- Enjoy **quick delivery**
- Purchase from **multiple local shops in one order**
- Combine convenience with familiarity

---

## Why Shopkeepers Use CityShop

- Digital presence without losing control
- Increased visibility within their own city
- Marketing support inside the platform
- No need to manage delivery logistics
- Compete with large e-commerce platforms fairly

---

## Impact

CityShop:
- Supports local businesses
- Preserves neighborhood economies
- Maintains customer trust
- Modernizes traditional commerce without replacing it
