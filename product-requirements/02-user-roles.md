## User Roles

### Customer

- Discover businesses
- Compare products and services
- View catalogs
- Book appointments
- Submit inquiries
- Contact businesses

---

### Business Owner

- Register business
- Manage profile
- Manage assigned modules
- Publish stories
- Publish advertisements
- Manage products
- Manage services
- Manage inventory
- Manage appointments

Capabilities depend on assigned modules.

---

### System Administrator

- Verify businesses
- Review documents
- Assign modules
- Manage subscriptions
- Moderate platform content
- Configure business access
