## Create CityShop

### Status
Verifying Database and Normalizing it. 

---

### Context

Local businesses are struggling to compete with large e-commerce and quick-commerce platforms.

Customers increasingly prefer the convenience of online ordering and fast delivery.

Many local businesses lack the technical resources to establish a digital presence.

---

### Decision

Create a city-wide commerce platform called CityShop.

The platform will connect:

- Customers
- Local Shopkeepers
- Delivery Partners

Customers will be able to order products from local shops through a single platform.

Shopkeepers will gain digital visibility and order management tools.

Delivery partners will handle order fulfillment and delivery.

---

### Expected Benefits

#### Customers

- Easy online ordering
- Fast delivery
- Access to local businesses

#### Shopkeepers

- Increased visibility
- Additional revenue channels
- Digital presence

#### Delivery Partners

- Flexible earning opportunities
